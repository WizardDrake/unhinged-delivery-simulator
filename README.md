# Unhinged Delivery Simulator
## Features
- 2 player local play
- 4 player P2P online play
- Upgrades
- Police
## How To Play (after getting the game)
- You use WASD to control player one(arrow keys for local P2)
- You go up to the houses (map show/hide with tab) and slow down.
- You then pick your upgrade with steering and space
- Then go back to the post office and slow down.
- Repeat
## Upgrades
- Radar (Upgrades your map)
- Nitro (lets you lock onto walls with space and go fast)
- Storage (lets you hold more packages)
